Stanley's List Of Near Purrfect B Movies

Stanley’s List of Favourite Fillums

Introduction

In no real order. Not really planning on defining b-movie but aiming for a vibe. Not big budget films, bit of a horror feel usually and an easy hook to describe it to someone.

Tremors ( & Tremors 2)

Giants worms devour a Chinese shop-keeper in a desolate American town.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/tremors & https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/tremors-ii-aftershocks

Starting with the best. Only the first two films are actually good. There is a hard to find spin off TV series and a dedicated fan base.

Deep Rising

Giant wormy things from the depths of the sea eat the rich while a ragtag bunch of petty crooks try to survive.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/deep-rising

Imagine if Ryan-air did cruise ships. They would be like this.

Deep Blue Sea

LL Cool J outlives Samuel L Jackson

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/deep-blue-sea

Super intelligent sharks learn how to open a giant can of humans. I believe there are circles but suspect they are awful.

Lake Placid

Official Golden Girls sequel, but with more Crocogator.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/lake-placid

There’s a crocodile. in a lake, that’s developed a taste for people. What more do you need. Possibly the best 82 minutes you’ll have.

Theatre of Blood

A ham actor gets his revenge on his cruel critics.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/theatre-of-blood

Is this a clever deconstruction of British class and society via the medium of 1970s horror? No. Best avoided if you love dogs.

The Monkey

Like the Final Destination films but with a toy monkey.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/the-monkey

Summary: See tagline.

The Colour Out of Space

Isolated farming family learn not to drink the water.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/color-out-of-space

Mainly here because it has Nicholas Cage and is a Lovecraft inspired film. I was tempted to put in Re-animator but that has not aged well at all.

Slither

Space slugs!

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/slither

Space slugs!!

Ghost Squad (ゴーストスクワッド )

Dead girls demand revenge

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/ghost-squad-2018

Again maybe not one to watch if you love dogs. I almost suggested the Virgin Psychics ( https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/the_virgin_psychics_2015 ) or The Happiness of the Katakuris ( https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/the-happiness-of-the-katakuris ).

If you like this you might like early noughties Japanese extreme gore but probably not.

Iron Sky ( and Iron Sky: The Coming Race if you must )

Moon Nazis invade Lady Trumps America.

Link: https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/iron-sky

So far into the so bad it’s good category it almost comes back round to bad again. The sequal has Hitler, Margaret Thatcher and Dinosaurs in it. Also a very unsubtle jab at iPhone fans.

Hook

I could have out in so many more films:

Kung Fu Hustle (https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/kung-fu-hustle) (Poor people fight kung-fu people resist crime-fu gangs) should probably replace Slither.

Jennifer’s Body (https://www.justwatch.com/uk/movie/jennifers-body) (Revenge of the zombie demon babe) should probably be up here too as a forgotten classic. It could replace Theatre of Blood?

Stanley.
