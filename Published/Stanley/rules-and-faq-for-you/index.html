Olaf Stapledon's List Maker

How does Stanley (me) make the list.

Releases

Bandcamp, YouTube & Tidal etc. Check the new release list regularly. If I listen to it at all it gets put on the release list. Normally an unlisted playlist on youtube.

I'm trying to filter out soundtrack songs as many of the asian dramas release a song per episode. Mando- and Canto- ballads tend to lose out here as I really don't know how to enjoy them. I like to hope there is another cat doing this.

Longlist

Anything that catches my ear that I want to listen to again gets put onto the monthly Longlist. This is my walk to work, daily listen playlist.

Songs released near the beginning of the month have an easier time getting onto the list. Catchy bass lines tend to do well. Quirky weird indie tracks often do well.

If I disocver AI I will remove the track and unsubscribe. I'm not perfect I might miss some use of AI. Remember VTubers and Voclaoids are not AI.

Final 10 and Runners Up

From about halfway though the month I'll have an idea what my favourite songs are and start filling up the Final 10 List.

They're in no particular order and I try to do a spread of styles. So if your amazing hard rock song ended up near the end of the list it might not make it through if I already have one or two.

Sometimes there is a batch of songs that seem to fit together and they often end up in the runner up section where they can huddle together like penguins.
