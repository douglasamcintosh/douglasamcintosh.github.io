1 How To Play

This game is not designed or aimed at novice GMs. It is my own notes on a setting I have used with my play group. Shared in a way that I hope other people may enjoy.
Cops; the muscular line between law and chaos. The boys in blue protect the city from the normal day to day criminals, busting heads and taking no prisoners Actually they do take prisoners. That’s the point. .

1.1 Statistics

Physical
Strength, dexterity it’s all here.
Wits
Cleverness and sense.
Presence
Personality. Persuasion, intimidation and cajoling.
Guts
Hunches, confidence.
Resilience
Physical toughness.Take a lickin’ and keep on tickin’.
Weird
Connection to The Dark. This also is a measure of the Dark’s connection to the character.
Cops are tough, clever and attuned to the world around them. To being with you get one +3 bonus, two +2 bonuses and two +1 bonuses.
