4 Notes

The world is not one. It is many and Other. At certain locations and times the walls between become thin. Stonehenge, Capel Mair ar y Bryn and Kilmarnock are all examples of places that are close to the Other.
In some places Innsmouth, Brichester and Dundee something has occurred that actually broken the wall. There are paths between, ghost walks, Fey trails, dead end underpasses that allow travel between. Unreality seeps in and the real world location becomes a little more distant. These locations at as a magnet for the weird and wonderful attracting people and things with an interest in the mysteries of the world. Most people still don’t notice, a person’s ability to lie to themselves is significant. Seen a ghost; ’I’ve had too much to drink’. Nosferatu in the shadows; ’trick of the light’. Chilling sense of potential apocalypse under a blood moon; ’bit chilly tonight I’ll put the central heating on!’
